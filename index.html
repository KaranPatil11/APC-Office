About Us

About Firm

Our intellectual property activity incorporates patents, trademarks, copyrights, designs, geographical indicators, and related IPR litigation (opposition and infringement).

We help respected businesses and individual owners manage, protect, and enforce their intellectual property portfolios in both contentious and non-contentious circumstances. We handle patent and trademark prosecution throughout India. Our firm represents both plaintiffs and defendants in a variety of intellectual property procedures.

The firm has extensive expertise in dealing with challenging problems in intellectual property registration and litigation of all kinds. It regularly handles petitions for intellectual property registration and protection, as well as disputed situations, in a number of settings, including all-level courts, patent and trademark offices, copyright and design offices, and the Intellectual Property Appellate Board.
